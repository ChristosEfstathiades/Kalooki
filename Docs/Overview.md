I am building a website where users can play the multiplayer card game kalooki also known as kaluki.
Users will be able to play with their friends in private matches as well as with strangers in public matches.
games will be in real time using websockets.
Kalooki is popular in the UK, Jamaica, and other countries such as cyprus.
There are lots of different variations of rules. All public matches on the website will use the standard rules defined by the website.
Private matches will be able to customise the rules.
Users need an account to play games.
The website doesn't just let users play games but provides other pages such as a how to play (rules) page, a tips and tricks page, a contact/support page, and a privacy policy page.
